# Homework 04 - Define Assertions from a FIFO Spec

## Assignment

Given a synchronous FIFO specification, identify at least 5 design rules that should become assertions.

## Your answer must include

- the original spec statement
- why it should be asserted
- what kind of violation it would catch
- whether you would also write a testcase for it

## Example format

| Spec statement | Assertion intent | Why important | Also test? |
|---|---|---|---|

## Stretch goal

Pick one assertion and explain whether it is a safety property or a protocol/property-style guarantee.
